Hobbies & Interests
Being Active
Keeping active & being in nature is my greatest passion.
Whether it be pilates, bike riding, swimming running or trying out a new dance class, I like to keep fit and be in the sunshine.
My Dog: Aria
I'm a proud pet parent to Aria, a 3 year old Rough Collie.
Aria's favourite things in life are chasing the tennis ball, watching butterflies and playing tug-of-war with socks.
Aria likes to explore & posts her escapades via insta: @arias.rough.life
Food & Wine
Passionate about great food and enjoy shopping for fresh and different produce at the markets.
I love trying new recipes at home and exploring new eating establishments.
Learning about fine and alternative wines intrigues me. I currently have a side hustle - working weekends at a natural wine bar, where I get to combine my passion for wine and being around people.
Travel
I've managed to see a good chunk of the world, discovering the colourful and diverse planet we live on.
A few highlights include:
- sleeping under stars in a Bedouin tent
- hiked Tiger Leaping Gorge in China
- white water rafted down Soca River, Slovenia
- volunteering on an organic farm in Estonia
- overnight train ride Moscow to St Peterburg
- riding a bicycle across Austria
- motor biking the Hai Van pass in Vietnam
- island hopping the Adriatic Sea
- spending Ramadan in Istanbul, Turkey
Volunteering
I make an effort to volunteer my time helping family & friends in need as well as joining with official volunteer organisations to assist with fundraising or washing laundry for people living rough.
Reading
Legal thrillers and spy novels are my favourite genre in fiction, authors in particular include John Grisham, John le Carre and Lee Child.

Recent non-fiction I have enjoyed include The Upside of Stress, Outliers, L'art de la Simplicite, Boys Will Be Boys and Barefoot Investor.
